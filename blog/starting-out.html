Starting out

April 27, 2021

My initial response to the passion project was a bit of confusion and thought, as I wasn't sure how I was to pursue something with everything going on in the world at the moment. After thinking a bit more I came up with some ideas...and now I am quite excited to get started. Choosing somewhere to start was quite easy for me, as I have a very large interest in technology and I have also been learning and working a lot to develop skills in the area over the last year. The difficult part was thinking of a medium to do this, and I am still not quite sure, as a lot of options that would have been available previously are not anymore.

Some things I've looked into are working with Janik Abdullayev from the Bluevale CI coding club. He has done a lot of great things with starting and teaching, as well as working with other students from the school to learn and solve problems. I think this would be a great oppertunity to offer my skills and help out. Something else that I was thinking of, in a different domain would be to help some small organization to simply build and deploy a website, or work on something of the sorts. Lastly, I also was looking into some organizations like Communitech and Jamhacks who are also helping the community in different ways, and I am planning on digging deeper into working with them.

I am excited to get started and see where this leads me, but I am a bit nervous as I don't like trying new things, and I have never done anything like this before. As for my vision, I hope that whatever I end up doing brings positivity to others, and others gain something from my final output. Can't wait to get started!
