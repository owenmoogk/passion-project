Goals

For this project, I want to pursue teaching in technology and using my skills to help others as I feel I am most skilled and abled in that domain, and I am most interested in it as well. I think that it would also be more feasible to bring it to the community than other skills during COVID-19.

Starting out

April 27, 2021

My inital response to the passion project was a bit of confusion and thought, as I wasn't sure how I was to pursue something with everything going on in the world at the moment. After thinking a bit more I came up with some ideas, and now I am quite excited to get started. Choosing somewhere to start was quite easy for me, as I have a very large interest in teachnology and I have also been learning and working a lot to develop skills in the area over the last year. The difficult part was thinking of a medium to do this, and I am still not quite sure, as a lot of options that would have been avalible previously are not anymore. I am excited to get started and see where this leads me, but I am a bit nervous as I don't like trying new things, and I have never done anything like this before. As for my vision, I hope that whatever I end up doing brings positivity to others, and others gain something from my final output. Can't wait to get started!
